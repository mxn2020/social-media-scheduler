# Social Media Scheduler

## Pages & Features

### 1. Dashboard
- Publishing calendar snapshot (this week)
- Post performance highlights
- Accounts connected status
- Queue count by platform
- Best time to post suggestions

### 2. Composer
- Create post with rich text, emojis, hashtags
- Multi-platform preview (Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook)
- Image / video upload & editing (crop, filters)
- Carousel / multi-image support
- AI caption suggestions
- Character count & platform limits indicator

### 3. Calendar
- Month / week / day view of scheduled posts
- Drag-and-drop rescheduling
- Filter by platform, status, campaign
- Color-coded by platform
- Time zone indicator

### 4. Queue
- Queue of upcoming scheduled posts
- Reorder posts in queue
- Pause / resume queue
- Auto-schedule (best times based on analytics)
- Bulk scheduling

### 5. Published Posts
- History of all published posts
- Post performance metrics (likes, comments, shares, reach)
- Sort & filter by platform, date, performance
- Re-share / repurpose published content

### 6. Drafts
- Saved drafts list
- Continue editing draft
- Convert draft to scheduled post
- Organize drafts by category

### 7. Analytics
- Follower growth per platform
- Engagement rate trends
- Best performing content types
- Optimal posting time analysis
- Audience demographics
- Platform comparison charts

### 8. Accounts & Platforms
- Connect social media accounts (OAuth)
- Account status (connected, expired, error)
- Per-account settings
- Multi-brand / workspace support

### 9. Hashtag & Trends
- Hashtag library (saved groups)
- Trending hashtags by platform
- Hashtag performance tracking
- Hashtag suggestions by topic

### 10. Team & Approval
- Team member roles (admin, publisher, contributor)
- Post approval workflow (submit → review → approve / reject)
- Comment & feedback on drafts
- Activity log

### 11. Settings
- Default posting times per platform
- Post templates
- Notification preferences (published, failed, mentions)
- Time zone configuration
- Billing & plan management
